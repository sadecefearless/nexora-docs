Nexora Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Nexora may store limited data required for bot functionality, including:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Configuration Settings
  • Leveling and Economy Data
  • Moderation Records

Nexora does not sell user data.

Nexora does not use collected data to train artificial intelligence models.

Users may request data deletion through the support server.
